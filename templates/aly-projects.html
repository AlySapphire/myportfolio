SpaceJam

Made with UE4 in less 24 hours as part of a game jam that my uni put on. You're a slug who is just sliding along, trying to avoid various obstacles that appear to the beat of the music by jumping up to the ceiling and falling to the floor. I worked on the movement and gravity flipping mechanics as well as collion detection and adding in the animations.

Languages/Engines Used: UE4, Blueprints

 OpenGL Demo

Made for my second year Graphics module at AIE Sydney. This is a small demo application that showcases some OpenGL techniques and graphics concepts I researched and implemented. Some tecniques shown in this application are Surface-based lighting with ambience and specular reflections, GPU-Accelerated particle system, Post-Processing effects such as Distortion and Sepia toning, Camera-frustum culling and loading an FBX model and animations and rendering them in 3D space using code.

Languages Used: C++, GLSL

 Nuts Bolts & Goblins (MAJOR WORK)

Nuts Bolts and Goblins is a game I developed in Unity3D with TeamPoliteDynamite for my first year major assignemnt at AIE Sydney. It's a side-scroller rhythm style game where you shoot different colored enemies with the corresponding buttons. I worked on the gameplay mechanics which involved shooting, weapon swapping, health and movement as well as the boss mechanics which included Ai programming, State machine, health and a physics simulation outside of Unity's physics engine.

Languages/Engines Used: C#, Unity3D
